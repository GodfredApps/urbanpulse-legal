6. YOUR DATA PROTECTION RIGHTS

Under the Ghana Data Protection Act, 2012, you have the following rights:

6.1 Right of Access

You have the right to know what personal data we hold about you and how we use it.

How to exercise: Email privacy@urbanpulse.app (Response within 30 days)

6.2 Right to Rectification

You can correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

How to exercise: Update your profile in the app or email us

6.3 Right to Erasure

You can request deletion of your personal data ("Right to be Forgotten").

How to exercise: Delete your account in the app or email privacy@urbanpulse.app

6.4 Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent for:

  • Marketing communications (use unsubscribe link)
  • Location tracking (device settings)
  • Optional data collection

6.5 Right to Object

You can object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.

6.6 Right to Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data in machine-readable format (JSON, CSV).

6.7 Right to Lodge a Complaint

If you believe we violated your rights, you can complain to:

Data Protection Commission of Ghana
Website: https://dataprotection.org.gh
Email: info@dataprotection.org.gh
Phone: +233 (0)302 684927/8

7. DATA SECURITY

We implement industry-standard security measures:

Technical Measures:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Controls: Role-based access, multi-factor authentication
  • Firewalls: Network protection against unauthorized access
  • Regular Audits: Vulnerability assessments and penetration testing

Organizational Measures:

  • Employee Training: All staff trained on data protection
  • Confidentiality Agreements: Employees sign NDAs
  • Incident Response: Procedures for handling data breaches

Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach:

  • We notify the Data Protection Commission within 72 hours
  • We notify affected users without undue delay
  • We provide information on consequences and measures taken
